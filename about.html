About Us | Our Story


  • Haven Courier is a global supplier of transport and logistics solutions, proudly based in the US. But our story isn't just about trucks and planes; it's about connections. It began in 1958 with a simple yet powerful idea: to bridge the gaps between businesses and their customers, no matter the distance. We envisioned a world where goods moved seamlessly, where deliveries were more than just packagesâ€”they were promises kept.

    In 1958, Haven Courier was founded with a single truck and a shared office. We weren't the biggest, but we were driven by a passion for precision and a commitment to personalized service. We understood that every shipment, no matter its size, represented someone's livelihood, someone's dream. Perhaps our founder and our team-members understood this better than anyone.

    That personal touch is still at the heart of everything we do, even after nearly seven decades. As we've grown, expanding our reach across the globe and incorporating cutting-edge technology, we've never lost sight of our core values: reliability, efficiency, and a genuine care for our clients. We're not just moving cargo; we're building relationships.

    At Haven Courier, we believe that logistics is more than just a process; it's a partnership. We work closely with our clients, understanding their unique needs and tailoring solutions that fit perfectly. We're not just a vendor; we're an extension of their team.

    Today, after 67 years of experience, Haven Courier stands as a trusted and respected name in the transport and logistics industry. We're proud of the vast network we've built, the innovative technology we've embraced, and most importantly, the enduring relationships we've nurtured. But our story is still being written. We're constantly striving to improve, to innovate, and to continue connecting businesses with the world, one delivery at a time. We invite you to join us on this journey.

Why Choose Us

  • Secure & Undamaged Delivery Package safety is paramount. We implement all necessary precautions to guarantee your shipments arrive safely and in perfect condition. To ensure secure, undamaged delivery, we utilize premium packaging materials, shielding items from transit damage. Careful handling is standard procedure, preventing any rough treatment that might cause harm.
  • Rapid & Reliable ShippingWe prioritize swift and dependable delivery for every client. Our goal is to offer trustworthy and effective shipping, surpassing both client expectations and industry benchmarks. To guarantee rapid, on-time delivery, we leverage cutting-edge technology and a team of expert professionals adept at managing diverse shipments. Our network of dependable partners and carriers ensures timely delivery to any destination.
  • Shipment Weather ProtectionWe recognize weather's impact on safe, timely deliveries. Despite meticulous planning, unforeseen weather can disrupt schedules and potentially damage shipments. To mitigate this, we offer optional weather protection. This safeguards against losses or damages caused by unexpected conditions like hurricanes, floods, heavy snow, and other severe weather events.
  • 24/7 Customer SupportOur customer service team is available 24/7 to address any queries or concerns. We are committed to providing exceptional service, ensuring that every client receives the support they need. Our team is always ready to assist, offering guidance and solutions to any shipping-related issues.
